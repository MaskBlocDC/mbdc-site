Service Area Map

Reference our service area map below to see if we take requests in your area: all areas marked in blue are where we currently service; all areas marked in red will be handed off to Mask Bloc NOVA to fulfill your request. If you live outside of our mapped areas, we are still able to schedule a pick-up if you are able to meet us within our service area. Please note that this is a living map, and areas may change as our volunteers' capacities fluctuate.
